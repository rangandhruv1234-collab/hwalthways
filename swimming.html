Olympic Standard

25-metre competition pool with 5 marked lap lanes, depth indicators and anti-turbulence ropes — built to match competitive conditions at every session.

Water maintained at 28°C regardless of the season. No cold-water shock, no off-season closures — swim in perfect comfort every single day of the year.

Nationally certified swimming coaches for all ages and levels. Your coach is on the pool deck every session — tracking your progress, refining your technique.

BUILT FOR
EVERY LEVEL.

Six structured swimming programmes designed to take you from first splash to peak performance. Small groups, expert instruction and morning or evening slots — available every day.

Beginner Adults

For adults swimming for the very first time. Learn water comfort, floating, breathing techniques and your first full strokes — in a calm, supportive small-group setting.

All Ages Beginner 45 Min
Kids (Age 3–12)

Refine your technique across all four strokes — freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke and butterfly. Covers lap building, turns and breathing patterns with focused correction every session.
