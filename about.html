The Founding Story

BORN FROM
TRUE BELIEF.

Healthways was founded in 1994 by Multitech Promoters with a single, unwavering belief: every person in Delhi deserves access to a world-class fitness facility. Not a franchise. Not a chain. A real gym — run by people who live and breathe fitness.

Starting with a compact but fully-equipped gym in Keshav Puram, we grew organically through word of mouth, reputation, and results. Today, our 15,000 sq ft campus houses five distinct facilities under one roof.

02 · The Journey

THREE DECADES
OF GROWTH.

From a single gym floor to one of Delhi's most complete fitness campuses — every milestone shaped by our members' trust and our commitment to getting better.

1994
The Doors Open
HEALTHWAYS launches in Keshav Puram, New Delhi. Founded by Multitech Promoters with a full weight training floor and certified coaching staff. The first members walked in and never left.
Late 1990s
The Pool is Born
A 25-metre competition-standard swimming pool is added, making HEALTHWAYS one of the few facilities in the area to offer both gym and aquatics under one roof. Children's and adult coaching programmes launch immediately.
2000s
Group Classes Expand
Aerobics, Zumba, Yoga, Dance and Fat Loss Functional Training are introduced. The group class floor becomes one of the busiest in North Delhi — morning and evening batches filling up daily.
2010s
Cafeteria & Full Campus
An in-house cafeteria is launched offering daily protein meals, smoothies and nutrition plans. At 15,000 sq ft, HEALTHWAYS becomes a full-day fitness destination — not just a gym you visit, but a place you spend time.
2024 →
WaysScore Coaching
HEALTHWAYS introduces WaysScore — a free AI-powered personal coaching engine. Members receive a personalised blueprint in minutes: daily meal plans, customised workout regimens and a WaysScore rating. Thirty years of wisdom, now digital.

05 · What We Stand For

PRINCIPLES
WE LIVE BY.

Thirty years in business teaches you what matters. These are the four principles that have guided every decision at HEALTHWAYS since day one.

01
Results, Not Rhetoric
We measure success by what changes in your body and mind — not by how many machines we have or how impressive our marketing looks.
02
Access for All
From the 6-year-old learning to swim to the 60-year-old returning to fitness — HEALTHWAYS was built to serve every age, every level and every goal.
03
Coaches, Not Gatekeepers
Our trainers are on the floor with you — not hiding behind an office desk. They correct your form, and help you become the best version of yourself.

Always Getting Better
From installing Delhi's best aquatic facility in the '90s to launching an AI coaching engine in 2024 — we have always invested in getting better.
